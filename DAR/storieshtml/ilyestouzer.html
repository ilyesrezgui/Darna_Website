Have your camel stop at this oasis in southwestern Tunisia, home to no less than 200,000 palm trees. Long prosperous thanks to its strategic location on the caravan route crossing the Sahara, Tozeur is renowned for its excellent Deglet Nour dates, its beige brick architecture and its 14th century medina (the Ouled El Hadef), where the fascinating Dar Cheraït museum which houses many archaeological objects, including jewelry and ceramics. Tozeur has also served as the setting for the Star Wars series of films.
